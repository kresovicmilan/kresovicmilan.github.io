Stay far but watch together with Koselig

Koselig is an AI-assisted browser extension for watching videos together on multiple platforms in just 2 clicks.

Get Started

Introduction

How does it work?

Step 1

To install Koselig, start off by clicking the "Install Koselig" button on the Chrome Web Store. After installing Koselig, you should see button next to your address bar.

Get the extension!

Step 2

Go to a streaming site and open a video. To create your party, click on icon located next to the address bar and share the party URL to invite friends.

Step 3

To join a party click on the party URL, which will redirect to the streaming website. Then click on button next to the address bar, and you should automatically join the party.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Which browser and devices are compatible with Koselig?

    Koselig is a browser extension that is available on all browsers with Chromium engine such as Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, and Opera browsers. Koselig only runs on laptop and desktop computers.

  • Is Koselig free?

    Koselig works on a Freemium model. All the basic features are completely free.

  • What is the limit on the number of people who can join Koselig at once?

    Up to 50 friends can participate in the same party session.

  • Is Koselig safe?

    We use end-to-end encryption and we do not store your streaming website’s account details or password.

  • Is voice or video chat available?

    Yes, Koselig supports only text-based chat and voice-based chat.

  • Run into a bug?

    Please re-install it to make sure you have the latest version which may have the bug fix. If the issue still persists, please email us at: koselig@mailinator.com
